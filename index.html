Let's start with Character Development. During character development you need to start with a character profile which is the beginning of the character's growth. The story that your creating is the growth of the character.

Start with the physical description. How old is the character? Gender? Who are they in the story? Are they a teacher, college student, or maybe a parent? What's their role and personality.

When it comes to the character their backstory is essential on why they act the way that they do. Ask questions like what changed their opinions on things after a certain experience.

This is basically the Moment That Everything Changed.

The Moment That Changed Everything is all about when the character was at a young age what made them think differently of the world.
